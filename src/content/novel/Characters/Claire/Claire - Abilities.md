Passive:  Static

A constant internal noise with two layers. The baseline is a low static hiss, always present, fluctuating in intensity with her emotional state. Beneath it are intrusive voice fragments—overlapping, half-formed warnings and accusations that describe only failures. 

Active: Visual Calculus

Allows Claire to see trajectories—the predicted paths of objects, people, and systems through space and time—as visible overlays of light. Most reliable with physical data (bullets, movement). Less reliable with human behavior. Useless on thoughts, emotions, truly random events, or herself. An internal click sound marks the moment the trajectory locks into certainty. A metabolic crash occurs scaling with complexity of the calculation.

Cost: Metabolic crash (glucose depletion → tremors → collapse).

Advanced – Certainty Field: A small zone of quiet certainty; no metabolic crash. Practically looks like “ultra instinct” where projectiles entering her zone are near instantaneously reacted to.

Cost: transforms to emotional regulation — the zone destabilises if she panics.
