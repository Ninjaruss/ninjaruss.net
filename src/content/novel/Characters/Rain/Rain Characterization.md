Main Trait: Procrastinator

Passive Trait: Dreamer

“The Paralyzed Dreamer”


| Layer | Expression | Bright/Dark | Transparent/Obscure |
| :----- | :----- | :----- | :----- |
| Layer 1 (Immediate) | “I’m fine”. Quiet, polite, deflective. | Slightly dark | Transparent |
| Layer 2 (Situational) | Avoids commitment. Analysis paralysis. Clone as proxy for actual action. Cannot act until he feels the decision is authentically his — a feeling that never arrives before the act, only after. Waits for alignment that commitment itself creates.  | Mid-bright | Obscure |
| Layer 3 (Foundational) | Believes his potential is his truest self. He will tell you he wants to create something meaningful — that part is visible. What he won't tell you is that his worth is staked on the outcome.  The untested dream is perfect; committing might prove he was never special. The dream is stated.  | Very bright | Very obscure |

His active ability involves sending a proxy of himself to do an action he’d like to do, but is intangible and cannot actually do anything, which relies on him to do the actions himself. This is an expression of the procrastinator trait.

While his passive ability is the ghost that guides him towards what he truly wants to do in spite of any risks or consequences. This is an expression of the dreamer trait.

He learns to throw things as his combat style. Throwing traps, utility items, etc. he acts from a distance and avoids direct combat.
