\#\#\# Shiori

\#\#\#\# Identity & Role

\- Tag: The Fragmented Recorder

\- Main Trait: Invasive

\- Passive Trait: Forgetful

\- Age: 22–24

\- Height: 5’5” or 5’8”?

\#\#\#\# Abilities

\- \*\*Passive – Afterimage:\*\* \*(Tentative)\* She sees the emotional residue of recent memories as faint colors/sensations around people? Not sure yet.

\- No active ability. No artifact. She compensates with gadgets.

\- Her gadgets are her external memory. She constantly attempts to record the present moment as she often forgets what has recently occurred.

\#\#\#\# Character Core

\- Her memory condition was caused by an early, imprecise extraction by Vesper. Stress worsens it; calm routines stabilize it.

\- Terror of disappearing. Hidden journal of terrible, sincere poems.

\- One detail she remembers without notes: Rain's nervous laugh.

\#\#\#\# Layers

\- Layer 1 (Immediate): Restless, probing, asks the wrong questions. Bright, extremely transparent.

\- Layer 2 (Situational): Builds janky gadgets to record, track, and remember; iterates relentlessly. Mid‑bright, semi‑transparent.

\- Layer 3 (Foundational): Terror of disappearing. Hidden journal of thoughts and poems. Very dark, very obscure.
