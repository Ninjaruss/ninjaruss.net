## Where are the real weebs? 
This seems like a dumb question, but it is one I find myself asking to find the core root of a true anime fan. If it wasn't appearent from the recent trend of calling people LARPers if they are pretending to like weeb stuff, there seems to be a growing rise of discerning the fake clout chasing normies from the true authentic people.
